## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Registration

1. Open the EduConnect app or navigate to the web portal.
2. On the Login screen, click **"Sign Up"**.
3. Complete the **3-step registration**:

   **Step 1 — Account Info:**
   - Enter your **Email**, **Username**, and **Password** (min 6 characters).
   - Select **"Teacher"** as your role.
   - Enter the **Institution Code** (required for teacher registration).

   **Step 2 — Personal Details:**
   - Enter your **First Name** and **Last Name** (required).
   - Optionally add **Phone** and **Date of Birth**.

   **Step 3 — Academic Info:**
   - Enter your **Employee ID**.
   - Enter your **Qualification** (e.g., M.Sc. Physics).
   - Enter your **Specialization** (e.g., Quantum Mechanics).

4. Click **"Register"**.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> After registration, your account requires **admin approval**. You will see the message: *"Registration successful! Please wait for admin approval."* You cannot log in until approved.

### 1.2 Logging In

1. Enter your **Email Address** and **Password**.
2. Click **Sign In**.
3. You will be redirected to the **Teacher Dashboard**.

### 1.3 Forgot Password

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the Login screen.
2. Enter your registered **email address** → Click **Send Verification Code**.
3. Enter the **6-digit OTP**, your **new password**, and **confirm** it.
4. Click **Reset Password**.

## 5. Attendance Marking

Navigate to **Attendance** in the sidebar. This is one of the most-used teacher features.

### 5.1 QR Code Attendance

The fastest way to mark attendance:

1. Select the **Course/Class** from the dropdown.
2. Select the **Subject**.
3. The system generates a **unique QR code** for the session.
4. Students scan the QR code using their mobile device to mark themselves present.
5. The student list auto-updates as students scan.

### 5.2 Manual Attendance

1. Select the **Course** and **Subject**.
2. The full student roster appears.
3. Check/uncheck each student as **Present**, **Absent**, or **Late**.
4. Click **Submit Attendance** to save the session.

### 5.3 Attendance Edit Requests

If you need to correct a past attendance record:

1. Submit an **edit request** specifying:
   - The **date** and **session**
   - The **student** whose status needs changing
   - The **proposed new status** (Present/Absent/Late)
   - A **reason** for the change
2. The request goes to the **admin** for approval.
3. Once approved, the attendance record is automatically updated.

> [!NOTE]
> Each attendance session records the **verification method** used (QR scan or Manual) for transparency.

## 6. Exam Management

Navigate to **Exams** in the sidebar.

### 6.1 Creating an Exam

1. Click the **create/add button**.
2. Fill in the exam details:
   - **Exam Title** (e.g., "Mid-Term Physics Exam")
   - **Subject** (select from your subjects)
   - **Course/Class**
   - **Date and Time**
   - **Total Marks**
   - **Duration**
3. Click **Save** to create the exam.

### 6.2 Editing & Deleting Exams

- Click the **edit icon** to modify exam details.
- Click the **delete icon** to remove an exam.
- Students will see the exam schedule in their Exams page.

## 7. Result Entry

Navigate to **Results** in the sidebar.

### 7.1 Individual Result Entry

1. Select the **Exam** from the dropdown.
2. The enrolled students list appears.
3. Enter **marks obtained** for each student.
4. The system automatically calculates:
   - **Percentage**
   - **Grade** (A+, A, B+, B, C, etc.)
   - **Pass/Fail** status
5. Click **Submit Results**.

### 7.2 Bulk Result Entry

For entering results for many students at once:

1. Select the **Exam**.
2. Use the **Bulk Entry** mode.
3. Enter marks for all students in the table.
4. Click **Submit All** to save all results at once.

## 8. Lesson Planner

Navigate to **Lesson Plans** in the sidebar.

### 8.1 Creating a Lesson Plan

1. Click the **create button**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** (e.g., "Chapter 5 — Newton's Laws")

- View all your lesson plans in a list.
- **Edit** any plan to update content.
- **Delete** plans that are no longer needed.

## 9. AI Summary Generator

Navigate to **AI Summary** in the sidebar.

### 9.1 Generating Summaries

1. Enter a **keyword or topic** (e.g., "Photosynthesis", "Algebraic Expressions").
2. Click **"Generate Summary"**.
3. The AI generates a comprehensive summary of the topic.
4. The summary appears with a **typewriter animation** effect.

### 9.2 Use Cases

- Prepare concise topic overviews for students.
- Generate quick reference material for revision classes.
- Create supplementary notes for difficult topics.

> [!NOTE]
> This feature uses **Google Gemini AI** for summary generation. Ensure the backend has a valid API key configured.

## 10. Study Materials & Question Papers

### 10.1 Study Materials

Navigate to **Materials** in the sidebar.

1. Click the **upload button** to add new study materials.
2. Upload files (PDF, DOC, PPT, images, etc.).
3. Add a **title**, **description**, and link to a **subject/course**.
4. Students can download these materials from their Materials page.

### 10.2 Question Papers

Navigate to **Question Papers** in the sidebar.

- Upload **past question papers** for student reference.
- Organize by subject and exam type.
- Students access these through their Question Papers page.

> [!TIP]
> Upload materials early in the semester so students have ample time for preparation.

## 12. Announcements

Navigate to **Announcements** in the sidebar.

### 12.1 Creating Announcements

1. Click the **create button**.
2. Enter the **title** and **content**.

## 13. Office Hours

Navigate to **Office Hours** in the sidebar.

### 13.1 Setting Up Office Hours

1. Define your available **consultation time slots**.
2. Set the **day**, **start time**, and **end time** for each slot.
3. Students can then **book** these slots for meetings.

### 13.2 Managing Bookings

- View all booked slots and the students who booked them.
- **Approve** or **cancel** bookings as needed.

## 15. Profile & Settings

### 15.1 Profile Page

Click your **avatar** in the sidebar bottom or navigate to **Profile**.

**View Your Profile:**
- Avatar, name, email, and role badge
- Employee ID and department
- Qualification and specialization
- Phone number and date of birth

**Edit Your Profile:**
1. Click **"Edit Profile"**.
2. Update fields: First/Last Name, Phone, Date of Birth, Employee ID, Department, Qualification, Specialization, Address.
3. Click **"Save"**.

**Change Profile Picture:**
- Click on your **avatar image** → Select a new image file (max 5MB).

**Change Password:**
1. Switch to the **"Change Password"** tab.
2. Enter your **Current Password** and **New Password**.
3. Click **"Change Password"**.

### 15.2 Settings

Accessible from the top navigation bar.

| Tab | Features |
|-----|----------|
| **Appearance & Theme** | Switch between **Light Mode** and **Dark Mode** |
| **Account Security** | Change your login password |
| **Notification Rules** | Toggle on/off: Announcements, Exam Schedules, Results, Office Hours Bookings, Email Digest |
| **Session & Meta Info** | View your User ID, email, role, approval status, and join date |

### 15.3 Logging Out

- Click the **Logout** button in the top navigation bar.
- You will be redirected to the Login screen.
