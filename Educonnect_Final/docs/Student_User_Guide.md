## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Creating Your Account

1. Open the EduConnect app or navigate to the web portal.
2. On the Login screen, click **"Sign Up"**.
3. Complete the **3-step registration process**:

   **Step 1 — Account Info:**
   - Enter your **Email Address**
   - Choose a **Username**
   - Create a **Password** (minimum 6 characters)
   - Select **"Student"** as your role
   
   > [!NOTE]
   > Students do **not** need an Institution Code. Only teachers and admins require one.

   **Step 2 — Personal Details:**
   - Enter your **First Name** and **Last Name** (required)
   - Add your **Phone Number** (optional)
   - Enter your **Date of Birth** (optional)
   - Enter your **Guardian Name** and **Guardian Phone** (optional but recommended)

   **Step 3 — Academic Info:**
   - Select your **Stream** (e.g., Science, Commerce, Arts)
   - Enter your **Section** (e.g., A, B, C, D)
   - Enter your **Enrollment/Roll Number**

4. Click **"Register"** to submit.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> After registration, you'll see the message: *"Registration successful! Please wait for admin approval."* Your account must be **approved by an administrator** before you can log in. This usually happens within 24 hours.

### 1.2 Logging In

1. Enter your **Email Address** and **Password**.
2. Click **"Sign In"**.
3. You'll be taken to your **Student Dashboard**.

### 1.3 Forgot Password

If you forgot your password:

1. On the Login screen, click **"Forgot Password?"**
2. Enter your **registered email address**.
3. Click **"Send Verification Code"**.
4. Check your email for a **6-digit verification code (OTP)**.
5. Enter the code, your **new password**, and **confirm** the password.
6. Click **"Reset Password"**.
7. You'll be redirected to the Login page — sign in with your new password.

> [!TIP]
> If you don't receive the code, click **"Resend Verification Code"** to get a new one.

### 1.4 Navigation Overview

After logging in, you'll see the **sidebar menu** on the left side of the screen:

|-----------|-------------|
| 🏠 Dashboard | Your personalized academic overview |
| 📢 Announcements | Read college-wide notices and updates |

## 2. Student Dashboard

The Student Dashboard is your **personalized homepage** showing your academic status at a glance.

### 2.1 Statistics Cards

At the top, you'll see **4 stat cards**:

| Card | What It Shows |
|------|---------------|
| **Enrolled Courses** | Total number of courses you're enrolled in |
| **Attendance** | Your attendance percentage and count (e.g., "85% — 34/40 classes"). Shown in 🟢 green if ≥75%, or 🔴 red if below |
| **Overall Grade** | Your overall academic grade (e.g., "A") with average percentage |
| **Subjects Passed** | How many subjects you've passed out of total (e.g., "5/6") |

### 2.2 Attendance Overview (Pie Chart)

A **donut chart** showing your attendance split:
- 🟢 **Green** = Classes attended (Present)
- 🔴 **Red** = Classes missed (Absent)

A legend below the chart explains the colors.

### 2.3 Recent Results (Area Chart)

A **performance trend chart** plotting your recent exam scores as percentages. This helps you visualize your academic progress over time.

### 2.4 My Courses

A list of all courses you're enrolled in, showing the **course title** and **course code** with a colored icon.

### 2.5 Recent Announcements

The latest announcements from your college, showing:
- Announcement **title**
- **Date** it was posted
- **Priority badge** (if high priority, shown in red)

## 5. Attendance

Navigate to **My Attendance** in the sidebar.

### 5.1 Viewing Your Attendance

- See your **overall attendance percentage** prominently displayed.
- View a breakdown of attendance by subject.
- Each record shows:
  - **Date** of the class
  - **Subject** name
  - **Status** (Present ✅ / Absent ❌ / Late ⚠️)
  - **Method** used for verification (QR Scan or Manual)

### 5.2 QR Code Attendance

When your teacher uses QR-based attendance:

1. The teacher will display a **QR code** on screen.
2. Open the **EduConnect app** on your phone.
3. Your **Student ID QR Code** (found on your Profile page) is used for identification.
4. Scan the teacher's QR code or have your QR code scanned.
5. Your attendance is **instantly marked** as "Present".

> [!IMPORTANT]
> You must be **physically present** in the classroom for QR attendance. The system records the verification method for transparency.

### 5.3 Attendance Statistics

- Track your attendance trend over the semester.
- A color-coded indicator shows your standing:
  - 🟢 **Green** (≥75%) — Good attendance
  - 🔴 **Red** (<75%) — Low attendance, needs improvement

> [!WARNING]
> Most colleges require a **minimum 75% attendance** to be eligible for exams. Keep your attendance above this threshold!

## 14. Profile & Settings

### 14.1 Your Profile

Click your **avatar/name** at the bottom of the sidebar, or navigate to **Profile**.

**Your Profile Card shows:**
- Your **avatar photo** (click to change)
- **Full name** and **email**
- **Role** badge (Student)
- **Enrollment/Roll Number**
- **Department** and **Stream**
- **Section**
- **Phone** and **Date of Birth**
- **Guardian** name and phone number

**Student ID QR Code:**
- Your profile includes a unique **QR Code** at the bottom.
- This QR code is your **Student ID** for QR-based attendance.
- Present this QR code to your teacher when they mark attendance via QR scanning.

### 14.2 Editing Your Profile

1. Click **"Edit Profile"** on the Profile page.
2. You can update:
   - First Name / Last Name
   - Phone Number
   - Date of Birth
   - Roll/Reg Number
   - Stream Department
   - Academic Stream
   - Class Section
   - Guardian Name & Phone
   - Address
3. Click **"Save"** to apply changes.

### 14.3 Changing Your Profile Picture

1. On your Profile page, **click on your avatar image**.
2. Select a new photo from your device (max **5 MB**).
3. The photo uploads automatically.

### 14.4 Changing Your Password

1. On the Profile page, switch to the **"Change Password"** tab.
2. Enter your **Current Password**.
3. Enter your **New Password** (minimum 6 characters).
4. Click **"Change Password"**.

### 14.5 Settings

Navigate to **Settings** (accessible from the top navigation bar).

| Tab | What You Can Do |
|-----|-----------------|
| **Appearance & Theme** | Choose between **Light Mode** ☀️ or **Dark Mode** 🌙 |
| **Account Security** | Change your login password with full validation |
| **Notification Rules** | Turn on/off notifications for: Announcements, Exam Schedules, Results Published, Office Hour Bookings, Weekly Email Digest |
| **Session & Meta Info** | View your User ID, email, portal role, account status (Approved/Pending), and date joined |

### 14.6 Logging Out

- Click the **Logout** button in the top navigation bar.
- You'll be taken back to the Login screen.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: I registered but can't log in. What should I do?
**A:** After registration, your account needs **admin approval**. Wait for the admin to approve your account (usually within 24 hours). If it takes longer, contact your college administration.

### Q: How do I mark my attendance?
**A:** Attendance is marked by your **teacher**. When the teacher uses QR attendance, scan the displayed QR code or present your Student ID QR code (from your Profile page). Manual attendance is marked directly by the teacher.

### Q: Where can I find study materials?
**A:** Go to **Materials** in the sidebar. All materials uploaded by your teachers for your enrolled courses will appear there. You can download them for offline study.

### Q: How do I check my exam schedule?
**A:** Navigate to **Exams** in the sidebar to see all upcoming and past exams with dates, times, and subjects.

### Q: Can I change my stream or section?
**A:** Yes, go to **Profile** → Click **"Edit Profile"** → Update your Stream and Section → Click **"Save"**. Note: Some changes may need admin approval.

### Q: How do I switch to Dark Mode?
**A:** Go to **Settings** → **Appearance & Theme** tab → Click on the **Dark Theme** card.

### Q: I forgot my password and don't have access to my email. What do I do?
**A:** Contact your college administrator to reset your account password manually.
